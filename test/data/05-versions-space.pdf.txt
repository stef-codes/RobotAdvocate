

Versioning Agreement
Effective Date: April 18, 2025
This  Versioning  Agreement  (the  "Agreement")  is  made  between  the  parties  involved  in  the
development,
maintenance, and distribution of documents, codebases, and other version-controlled content.
1. Definitions
1.1 "Version" means any identifiable state of a document or software after a change is recorded.
1.2 "Repository" refers to a storage location where multiple versions are tracked and managed.
2. Scope of Versioning
2.1 Each version must be uniquely identifiable and include a timestamp and author information.
2.2 The parties agree to maintain version integrity and avoid unauthorized alterations.
3. Responsibilities
3.1 Contributors are responsible for accurate and complete documentation of changes.
3.2 Reviewers must ensure compliance with version control standards before approvals.
4. Legal Implications
4.1 Breach of this Agreement may lead to legal recourse including, but not limited to,
termination of access to version control systems and potential litigation.
This Agreement is entered into voluntarily and with full understanding of its terms.

Signed,
Version Control Administrator
